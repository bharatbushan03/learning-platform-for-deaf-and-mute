Imagine you have a friend who cannot speak or hear.

When you want to talk to him, you use your hands making special shapes and movements.
This is called sign language. Its like a secret hand code that deaf and mute people use to communicate.

But here's the problem :- 
Most people don't understand this hand code.
So your friend is stuck. He's talking with his hands, but nobody around hims understands what he's saying. It's like being in a room full of people and screaming but nobody can hear.

And right now, there's no easy, fast tool that can watch hands and instantly tell others what he's trying to say.

That's the problem.

A deaf person's words are trapped inside their hands and the world doesn't know how to read them.
